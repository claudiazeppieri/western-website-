English students have a prodigious capacity to communicate with given texts. They inherit robust writing skills, can convey information to the highest degree, and can then become analagous in how they present their own works. Through the examination and engagement with literary text, students acquire highly developed communication skills that are necessary in the workplace. In doing so, they will have frames of confidence that distinguishes them from opposing bodies. They then can choose from numerous fields and build a successful career.

With an English degree, one can choose from a wide variety of professions that pertains to their own interests. Some of these include having a career in: Publishing, Journalism, Public Relations, Education, and Writing.
